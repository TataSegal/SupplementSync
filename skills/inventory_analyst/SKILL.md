# Skill: Inventory Analytics

Analyzes supplement intake compliance, habits, and schedules replenishment timelines.

## Instructions
- Compute adherence score: taken divided by total scheduled doses.
- Identify patterns in missed doses (e.g. morning vs evening compliance).
- Project bottle depletion dates based on daily dosage schedules and remaining stock.
- Generate warning reports when a supplement's stock falls below a 7-day reserve.
